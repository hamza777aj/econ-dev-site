The Surprise Economic Development team is committed to the constant pursuit of attracting quality jobs and business to Surprise and the Greater Phoenix Area.


Why are people and businesses voting with their feet and moving to the City of Surprise?

Surprise, Arizona, northwest of Phoenix, has leaped to the top ranks of new American cities and is attracting widespread attention.

With a growing population of 110,000, Surprise continues to be recognized as one of the top "North American Cities of the Year" by Foreign Direct Investment (fDi) magazine.

Creative Partnerships

Surprise is open to collaborations that work for everyone. Our partners include the University of Advancing Technologies (UAT), the Arizona Small Business Development Center (AZ SBDC) and Service Corps. of Retired Executives (SCORE). We are also strategically aligned with Rio Salado Community Colleges, which via their local "Communiversity" will be offering workforce training as well as Associates, Bachelors, Masters and even PhD degrees.

Surprise is home to Spring Training for the Kansas City Royals and the Texas Rangers as well as professional tennis. 75% of homes in Surprise were built after the year 2000. Surprise City Center offers: 5 acre urban fishing lake, 2 dog parks, the West Valley branch of the Heard Museum, an aquatic center, Dream Catcher Park, a disability-accessible safe-play sports area for children, and a new City Hall (move in mid-June 2009) and Public Safety Building. New shopping and restaurant amenities, including both local "mom-and-pop's" and high-end.
