(M)edical Travel

Arizona is home to numerous hospitals ranging from large, urban medical centers to small, community healthcare facilities. The Mayo Clinic, Dignity Health and Banner Health are just a few of the major players in the state. Collaborative partnerships have thrived, seamlessly meshing universities, research institutes and hospitals. Between Arizona's three public universities, 67 bioscience start-up companies have been formed with over 180 bioscience patents. Biomedical jobs are growing three times faster in Phoenix than the national average, with 20,000 jobs created in a three year span.
